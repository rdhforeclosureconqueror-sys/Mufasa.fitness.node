How you use Pocket PT

A simple daily loop from goal to next session.

Tell Pocket PT about your goals.

Sign in and complete intake, goals, and check-in steps when prompted.

Choose or receive a workout.

Select a starter template, open the current program, or use an exercise from the library.

Connect the camera when needed.

Use movement tracking for supported exercise patterns and challenge mode.

Complete the work.

Pocket PT tracks reps in supported flows and shows cues while you train.

Review progress and history.

Open the dashboard to see completed sessions, consistency, and active workout status.

Return or ask a question.

Continue from your training flow or ask a practical workout, recovery, or mobility question.

Personalization over time

One place for goals, history, reps, check-ins, and performance.

Pocket PT keeps your goals, workout history, and performance information together so your training can become more personalized over time.

Current personalization uses information from profile, intake, goals, completed workouts, check-ins, selected programs, and supported movement performance. Advanced automatic plan adaptation should be treated as a future growth area, not a live guarantee.

Safety first

For pain, injury, medical conditions, or urgent concerns, consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Pocket PT is a fitness guidance and accountability system. It does not diagnose injuries, replace a doctor, replace a physical therapist, or replace a registered dietitian.
